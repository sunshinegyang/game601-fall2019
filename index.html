My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2019

Game Modification: Number Guessing Game
Through trial and error, the designers tried to change the rules to add challenges, competition and life to a simple game. They repeated this process of playtesting and theorizing possible problems that the players would run into while playing the adapted numbers game. In making the game, they attempted to make it simple and easy to understand as to not deviate from the original concept of the overall game.
PLAY
(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: CAREER in America
This is an educational board game where players are able to dive into different identities in different fields of their career. Resources are limited to gender and cultural-based realities in America and tailored to each specific career determined by the players.
PLAY
Visual Novel or Interactive Fiction: Ava's Simulation
Ava is thrusted into a dimension where she has no recollection of her memories. She is forced to live through simple day-to-day tasks, not knowing how dangerous it is even if it means just touching a cute cat.
PLAY
(Final) Digital Game: Amélie
Orchestrating the lives of characters around her, innocent and naive amélie finds herself in a world , exclusively of her own making. An interactive digital narrative through the lens of characters amélie and partner, nino, comes a web of clever decision-making and roadblocks accompanied with stills and videos throughout the story. The wrong decision and you can miss data & information to fully complete the game.
